About Me

Hello and welcome to my world of three-dimensional creation! I'm Daniel Rivera, a passionate 3D modeling artist with a love for turning concepts into stunning digital artwork.

My journey in 3D modeling began in 2020 when I was captivated by the skill of artists like Khyzyl Saleem, Ash Torp, and Jon Sibal in bringing vehicles to life in the virtual space. Inspired by their masterpieces, I started exploring the amazing potential of tools like Blender.

With over three years of experience in the field, I've progressed from creating small models to tackling the challenge of modeling complete cars from scratch. Alongside fifteen personal projects, each project was a step towards perfection, and although my early attempts weren't flawless, I committed to constant improvement.

My skills grew with every new model, from a BMW E30 to a Honda Civic SI, where fidelity and realism were my main goals. The path to excellence meant embracing the learning curve, exploring new techniques, and taking on bold challenges.

A turning point came when I had the opportunity to model a Dragster Pro Mod for the game Assetto Corsa. Despite information limitations, my dedication and skills allowed me to capture every unique detail. Since then, I've completed 21 models from scratch, created over 26 components, and transformed existing models into unique visions.

My focus on quality, attention to detail, and passion for innovation has enabled me to attract clients and exceptional challenges. Each project is an opportunity to push boundaries and refine my art.

While my journey has been rewarding, I'm committed to ongoing improvement and growth. My goal is to offer exceptional service in the world of 3D modeling, and I'm excited to work with you to bring your ideas and visions to life in the digital space.

I invite you to explore my portfolio and join me on this exciting journey of creativity and three-dimensional creation!
