Skills

High Poly

High poly modeling involves creating 3D models with a high level of detail. This technique is commonly used when the final output requires intricate textures, realistic lighting, and close-up shots. By meticulously sculpting and adding a large number of polygons, the resulting model captures fine details, curves, and nuances, making it suitable for rendering photorealistic images and animations. High poly models are often used for showcasing design concepts, presentation materials, and marketing visuals.

Hard surface modelling

Hard surface modeling is a specialized technique used to create objects with well-defined, non-organic shapes and surfaces. It's particularly relevant in the realm of vehicles where many components have distinct edges, angles, and mechanical features. Hard surface modeling involves precision in crafting elements like car bodies, panels, mechanical parts, and architectural structures. This technique ensures that the final model accurately represents the mechanical and structural aspects of the vehicle.

Modelling from scratch

3D modeling from scratch involves creating a complete 3D representation of a vehicle or object without relying on existing references or templates. This service is all about transforming a concept or idea into a fully realized 3D model. Starting with sketches, rough outlines, or even just a description, the artist employs their creativity and technical skills to build the entire vehicle in the virtual space. This service is perfect for clients who have a unique design in mind that hasn't been visualized before or for those looking to explore innovative concepts.

Rendering

Rendering is the process of creating high-quality images or animations from your 3D models. It's the final step that brings your meticulously crafted 3D creations to life with realistic lighting, shadows, textures, and materials. Through rendering, your vehicle designs are transformed into visually stunning visuals that closely resemble real-world objects. This service is essential for producing captivating visuals for presentations, marketing materials, product showcases, and more. Whether you need static images or dynamic animations, rendering adds the finishing touch that enhances the overall impact of your 3D vehicle art.
