Turning a Concept into Reality: Creating a 3D Model from Minimal References

Embark on a journey with me as I take on the fascinating challenge of transforming a non-existent car concept into a 3D reality. With only six low-quality reference photos that resembled simple sketches, I accepted the challenge to bring the Mazda 6 promod to life. These references provided a basic idea of the design and body shape, offering a starting point for my creative journey.
