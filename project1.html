Mitsubishi Eclipse

PJ Mufflers

Description

In the exciting world of 3D vehicle modeling, I had the opportunity to take on an exceptional challenge: creating a 3D model from scratch for the renowned racing simulation game, Assetto Corsa. My focus was on the iconic Mitsubishi Eclipse, with the thrilling goal of transforming this vehicle into an exhilarating experience within the game.

Role and challenge

As an integral part of the team, my responsibility was to create a 3D model of the Mitsubishi Eclipse, based on reference photos and blueprints of the original vehicle. One of the main challenges was to modify the hood to achieve improved aerodynamics, following the features of the real vehicle. Additionally, I added essential elements for its function, such as an impressive rear wing, a custom exhaust system, strategically designed air intake, and an anti-wheelie bar.

Rear lights and Extior details

Immersed in the pursuit of excellence, I undertook the task of creating models of the rear lights, ensuring that each detail aligned with the authenticity of the vehicle. Every line and shape was carefully designed to capture the essence of the Mitsubishi Eclipse.

Interior Challenges

Inside the cockpit, I faced the challenge of representing the Roll Cage and front chassis with the limitation of having few reference photos. However, this challenge only strengthened my determination and creativity to achieve an accurate and realistic representation in the virtual environment.

Outcome and satisfaction

The final result of this project was a mod for Assetto Corsa that transformed the Mitsubishi Eclipse into a 3D masterpiece, with all aerodynamic and functional elements meticulously integrated. This project was not just about modeling a vehicle; it was about bringing the essence of the real world into the virtual world, offering players an exciting and authentic experience.

This project reinforced my passion for 3D modeling and my ability to tackle complex challenges. The Mitsubishi Eclipse PJ Mufflers Mod is a testament to my dedication to excellence and my ability to turn concepts into thrilling virtual realities.
