Featured Project: Creating a 3D Model - Mazda 6 Pro Mod

I took on an exciting challenge by creating a 3D model of the Mazda 6 Pro Mod for my client, Alex Cosme (also known as alex.racing.jr). Even though I only had access to six low-quality reference photos, I dived into making a car that didn't physically exist. Here, I'll show you how I did it step by step and the final results of this cool project.

Starting from Scratch: Bringing Ideas to Life

I began by working with the few reference photos to shape the car's front design. Using the right blueprints and detailed images of the original body, I started to capture the main look of the Mazda 6 Pro Mod.

Shaping the Body: The Challenge of the Back

Since the reference photos gave me only a rough idea of the back part, I needed to ask Alex if I could make a back part similar to the stock version. He said yes, so I went ahead. Every detail helped me get the right length and shape, and I tried my best to match what I saw in the pictures.

Adding Cool Features: Pro Mod Details

Pro Mod cars are all about the unique details that make them stand out. I added the special elements of the front, like lights, grills, and distinctive accessories. I had creative freedom to remake the front and rear lights with all their parts.

Creating Identity: Unique Spoilers and Accessories

I enriched the Mazda 6 Pro Mod's personality by making roll bars and a basic frame. I looked at other cars for inspiration and made each part with care. From the rear spoiler to the window details and exhaust system, each piece shows my commitment to making it look real.

Polishing and Presenting: The Power of Final Touches

Before showing the final version, I smoothed out the model by reducing unnecessary parts in the simpler elements. This made the overall design come together nicely and allowed the highlights to shine even more.

A Look at the Process: Pictures that Tell the Story

In the next section, you'll see a visual journey of how I did it. The pictures take you through the whole process, from the first sketches to the finishing touches, so you can see how the project came to life.

The End Result: Mazda 6 Pro Mod in All Its Glory

In the end, I created a Mazda 6 Pro Mod with passion and hard work. Every curve, every detail, and every texture come together to make a visual presentation that's meant to catch the eye of potential employers and clients alike.

This project shows my ability to tackle challenges and turn limitations into opportunities. Working with Alex, paying close attention to details, and loving the creative process all came together to make a 3D model that not only respects the original vision but also shows off my skills and creativity in the world of car design.
